About Us

We view the clinical researchers who use Medrio as partners in a common goal: to live in a stronger, healthier world.

At Medrio, our mission is to provide researchers in early phase pharma, medical device, and diagnostics clinical trials with the tools they need to accelerate their clinical data management and bring life-saving medical products to market. This means enabling our customers to access speed and simplicity over unnecessary complexity, all while reducing clinical trial costs and keeping their studies running smoothly. Our success is defined by our customers’ success. That’s why Medrio is the market-leading eClinical solution for early phase clinical trials.
